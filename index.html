Shanghai University of Finance and Economics

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Statistics and Data Science, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. I received my B.S. and Ph.D. in Statistics from Nankai University in 2019 and 2024, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Changliang Zou and Prof. Zhaojun Wang. From 2024 to 2026, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Nankai University.

Contact Information

Email: nk.xlcui@gmail.com or cuixiaolong@sufe.edu.cn

Research Interests

My research interests lie in changepoint detection, robust statistics, and extreme value theory.
